About

Click anywhere on the image to capture that pixel and convert the color to Forza Color.

Copy/paste an image into the canvas or load it from the IMG button.

Click anywhere on the picture to capture the color, if pixel size is larger than 1, it will calculate the average color.

Use SHIFT+MouseScroll to adjust the average pixel size.

LC to select a stored color, RC to remove it.

Added "Paste-Feature" by request from LanceMuscles from the Forza-Painter Discord server. Seriously check out that tool!!

The image isn't uploaded to the server, but simply applied on the client-side.

Support at Forza Motorsport Forums, /r/forza, Twitter/X, or DxBang on discord.
